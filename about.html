about me.


I am always learning. Whether that be through my school work or inventing small apps that make my life a little easier. My passion lies somewhere between technology and people. Hence, collaborative coder. As broad as that sounds, it's really simple.

Before college, I really didn't like working in groups, but I quickly learned that working with people brings me joy. I want to be in an environment that allows me to turn consumer demands into technical solutions. I thrive best when I'm able to be outgoing and encouraged to ask questions. Not only this, but I learn best through my struggles. I know I will make mistakes, but my willingness to succeed turns these mistakes into unprecedented growth.
